Purpose

Being yourself can be difficult when the daily commotion of life seems to beat you down to your knees. Sometimes simple yet unique reminders can give us that boost to represents the most important part of life. standing up and pushing beyond the gravity of daily mental health challenges. Staying yourself and being yourself daily. Simple yet little steps to making life easier and cozier with Alien wear a mental health clothing line .

Content

Home page

This is where the eye catching design comes in. This would be a minimalist look but with a fun design. A very strong yet short hook to get the user to click through the site more. This would be something about how we support a mental health/ charity and it goes towards that as we want to guide people to higher self confidence. With the minimal look i want to have a similar experience with the type on the page. so the hook has to be good enough to capture the idea but lead them to the other pages. About us page is the best place to go for more info on the mental health / charity. As well as the bigger picture vision statement of the company. Going into depth on the why of the business and merchandise.

Images for the Home page

Alien (merchandise)

This page will have a super simple carousel with product images and models with merchandise. There will be a dropdown with where you can navigate the clothing options easier. Very minimal wording on this page it will mostly contain the merchandise and images.

Images for the Page 2

About Us!

This page will have a large amount of the text for the entire site. It will go into detail about the reasoning behind the simplistic design. showing that not needing a large amount of flashy text can make you feel comfortable in any clothing with a minimalist logo. the Alien! This will also be a section explaining that it donates to a charity. This could possibly be a mental health organization. Also some major type will be present on the vision of the merch and company and proved a few messages from friends about their experiences being confident in their clothing.
